The Firm

About the Firm

Ombachi & Company Advocates is a full-service law firm based in Kisii, Nyanza, Kenya. We are duly admitted advocates of the High Court of Kenya, and hold the designation of Commissioner for Oaths and Notary Public.

Our firm was founded on a simple but powerful conviction: that every client deserves to be heard. Not processed. Not rushed. Heard. From that listening comes the action that resolves disputes, protects rights, and advances justice — in Kisii, across Kenya, and beyond.

We serve individuals, families, entrepreneurs, corporations, and institutions — bringing the same depth of commitment to a land title transfer as to a complex commercial dispute.

"Justice is not a privilege. It is a right we help you exercise."
