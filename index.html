About the Firm

A Firm Built on
Integrity & Purpose

Ombachi & Company Advocates is a Kisii-based law firm providing dynamic, ethical, and client-centred legal services across Kenya. We are registered as Advocates, Commissioners for Oaths, and Notary Public, serving individuals, families, and businesses across the region.

Our approach is grounded in the belief that every client deserves to be heard. We take time to understand the unique circumstances of each matter and craft legal strategies that are both principled and effective.

From our offices in Kisii, we serve clients across the region with the same depth of expertise and personal attention that has defined our firm since its founding.

Our Vision

To be a law firm that listens and provides dynamic, ethical, and sustainable legal solutions to the global community.

Our Mission

To help clients resolve legal problems through justice systems — listening to every client, understanding their issues, and providing personalised, innovative solutions for a sustainable just community.

What We Do

Areas of
Practice

We offer comprehensive legal services across a range of practice areas, combining deep expertise with a commitment to personalised, ethical representation for every client.

01
Conveyancing & Property Law
Sale and purchase of land, title transfers, lease agreements, searches, and all matters relating to real property transactions in Kenya.
02
Employment Law
Workplace disputes, wrongful termination, employment contracts, and representation before the Employment and Labour Relations Court.
03
Family & Succession Law
Divorce, custody, maintenance, adoption, probate, and the administration of deceased estates with sensitivity and discretion.
04
Commercial Law
Business formation, commercial contracts, partnership disputes, debt recovery, and legal advisory for businesses of all sizes.
05
Planning, Land & Environment
Land use, environmental compliance, compulsory acquisition disputes, and representation before the Environment & Land Court.
06
Commissioner for Oaths & Notary
Witnessing and certification of documents, statutory declarations, affidavits, and notarisation for local and international use.
